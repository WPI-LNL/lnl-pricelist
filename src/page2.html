Lighting Services
Choose one or multiple of Stage Lighting, Area Lighting, or Uplighting for your event. Then, choose any Add-ons. Rigging may be required; see the third page for details.
Stage Lighting
Illuminate your stage or performance area.
  • Basic
    $50
    Stationary stage lighting that may be dimmed as necessary. Great for small on-stage events.
  • Advanced
    $75
    Changing colors and moving lights. Great for larger events such as concerts or large on-stage performances.
Area Lighting
Lighting for an area other than a stage, such as a dance floor or dinner tables.
  • Basic
    $50
    May be statically colored and dimmed as necessary. Great for lighting a few tables or small areas.
  • Advanced
    $75
    Changing colors, moving lights, and effects. Great for dances.
Uplighting
$50
Uplighting to give your event an extra splash of color. Great for a wide variety of occasions.
Add-ons
  • Truss Mounting a Projector
    $20
    Hanging a projector from truss. The projector must be borrowed from the ATC.
  • Fog Machine
    $20
    Add fog to your event. Requires coordination with WPI Police for use indoors.
  • Special
    $10
    A single light illuminating a specific spot that is not already lit. Great for lighting a photobooth or a sign.
